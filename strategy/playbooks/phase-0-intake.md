# Phase 0: Intake & Scoping Playbook

> **MERIDIAN Phase 0** — The foundation of every engagement. Scope it right here and everything downstream is more efficient. Scope it wrong here and the engagement will drift, deliverables will miss the mark, and regulatory gaps will surface at the worst moment.

---

## Phase Overview

**Lead Agent**: MERIDIAN Orchestrator
**Duration**: 1–2 weeks
**Primary output**: Engagement scope document + regulatory trigger map + agent activation plan

---

## Step 1: Initial Client Briefing

### What to gather

- **Company profile**: sector (GICS), revenue, employee count, geography of operations, listed vs. private
- **Trigger**: what prompted this engagement? (regulatory deadline, investor letter, board decision, proactive leadership)
- **Prior work**: existing GHG inventories, sustainability reports, CDP submissions, materiality assessments — obtain and review before scoping
- **Known commitments**: SBTi committed? CDP signatory? RE100? TNFD adopter? TCFD reporter?
- **Timeline drivers**: what are the hard deadlines? CSRD first report? CDP submission? Board presentation?

### Key questions to ask

1. What does success look like for this engagement — one year from now?
2. What's the most urgent regulatory deadline driving this work?
3. Who is the primary internal owner? Sustainability team? CFO office? Legal?
4. What data do you have today? GHG inventory? Energy data? Supply chain spend data?
5. Who are the stakeholders who need outputs from this engagement?

## Step 2: Regulatory Trigger Mapping

### Mandatory framework assessment

Work through each jurisdiction and company characteristic:

**EU — CSRD/ESRS**
- Is the company an EU-listed company, large EU company, or non-EU company with EU nexus?
- Phase-in timeline: FY2024 reports (largest listed EU companies) → FY2025 (other large EU) → FY2026 (listed SMEs) → FY2028 (non-EU companies with €150M+ EU revenue and EU branch/subsidiary)
- CSRD = mandatory double materiality + ESRS disclosures + XBRL tagging + limited assurance

**EU — EU Taxonomy**
- Is the company a financial undertaking (NFRD-scope) or large non-financial under CSRD?
- Article 8 KPIs: turnover %, capex %, opex % — taxonomy-eligible and taxonomy-aligned

**EU — CSDDD (Corporate Sustainability Due Diligence Directive)**
- EU Directive 2024/1760 — applies to large companies (>1,000 employees, >€450M global revenue) and non-EU companies with significant EU revenue
- Human rights and environmental due diligence + civil liability

**US — SEC Climate Disclosure Rule (Rule 33-11275)**
- Note: rule is stayed pending litigation as of 2024
- Large Accelerated Filers: Scope 1+2 disclosure, Scope 3 if material, TCFD-aligned
- CA SB 253: Scope 1+2+3 for companies with $1B+ revenue doing business in CA (first reports 2026)
- CA SB 261: Climate financial risk reporting for $500M+ companies (first reports 2026)

**UK — TCFD / Streamlined Energy and Carbon Reporting (SECR)**
- Listed companies and large private companies: SECR mandatory (energy use, GHG emissions, energy intensity)
- UK-listed companies: TCFD-aligned disclosure in annual report (mandatory for premium-listed)

**CDP**
- Is the company a CDP signatory? Does it respond to CDP Climate, Water, or Forests?
- Is the company being scored by CDP (even without direct participation)?

**GHG Protocol / SBTi**
- Has the company committed to SBTi? Target set vs. committed vs. validated?
- Is SBTi commitment pending — near-term target required within 24 months of commitment

## Step 3: Sustainability Maturity Assessment

### Maturity levels

| Level | Characteristics | Typical Starting Point |
|---|---|---|
| **1 — Ad hoc** | No prior GHG inventory; limited data; no formal commitments | Start from Phase 1 scratch |
| **2 — Emerging** | 1–2 years of GHG data; basic ESG reporting; no verified data | Phase 1 with gap assessment |
| **3 — Developing** | Multi-year GHG series; CDP submission; partial Scope 3 | Phase 1 refinement + Phase 2 |
| **4 — Advanced** | Verified GHG data; SBTi committed; TCFD reporting | Phase 2 or 3 depending on gaps |
| **5 — Leading** | Validated SBTi targets; full Scope 3; CSRD/CDP A; third-party assured | Phase 3–4 continuous improvement |

### Maturity assessment checklist

- ☐ Prior GHG inventories: available? years covered? verified? methodology documented?
- ☐ Scope 3: screened? material categories quantified? DQS scores assigned?
- ☐ Sustainability report: published? framework (GRI/ISSB/TCFD)? assurance level?
- ☐ CDP: submission history? current score? module coverage?
- ☐ SBTi: status (not committed / committed / validated)? near-term target? net zero target?
- ☐ Data infrastructure: ESG platform? manual collection? audit trail?
- ☐ Internal governance: sustainability committee? board oversight? CSO?

## Step 5: Data Collection Initiation

### Data collection templates to issue

Issue the following to client data owners at Phase 0 — don't wait for Phase 1:

**Energy & Emissions**
- Utility bills or smart meter data (electricity, gas, heat/steam) — all facilities, all months
- Fuel purchase records (diesel, petrol, LPG, natural gas, refrigerants, aviation fuel)
- Production volume or throughput data for intensity metrics
- Facility list with: address, floor area, occupancy type, ownership vs. lease

**Supply Chain (Scope 3)**
- Total procurement spend by category (for spend-based Scope 3 Cat 1 estimate)
- Key supplier list with: spend, commodity, country of origin
- Business travel: air and rail records, car rental, expense reports
- Employee commuting: headcount by location, remote work policy

**Operations**
- Waste data by type and disposal route
- Water withdrawal and consumption by source and facility
- Business fleet: vehicle count, type, fuel, mileage

**ESG Social Metrics**
- Headcount: full-time, part-time, contract — by geography, gender
- Safety: recordable incidents, lost-time incidents, fatalities — by entity
- Pay data for gender pay gap and CEO pay ratio (if in scope)

### Data submission deadlines

Set deadlines with buffer:
- Data request issued: Phase 0 complete
- First submission due: Week 2 of Phase 1
- Final data locked: Before Phase 1 quality gate
